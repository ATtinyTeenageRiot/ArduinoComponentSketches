# ACS-85-0008
 ATTiny85 Squarewave with Vibrato

## Overview:

 Two inputs.  One for Freq and the other for Vibrato width.
 The outputs are
 PB0 - Frequency ramps down
 PB1 - Frequency Ramps Up
 PB2 - Frequency goes up and down as a Triangle wave
